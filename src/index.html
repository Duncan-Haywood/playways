About Playways

Playways is a music visualiser and generative art app by Katrina Hayes, a web developer in Sydney.

The visualisation works by continually measuring a song's amplitude and visualising each reading as a circle of a specific diameter, while also creating new circles at points when the current amplitude differs significantly from the previous. Every canvas comes out a little differently.

Listen to the preloaded songs by Ratatat or Elliot Smith, or switch to mic mode to create a visualisation from your environment—like music you're playing through your computer or sounds you create with your own voice/instrument.

If you create something interesting, take a screenshot and let me know. I'd love to see it!
